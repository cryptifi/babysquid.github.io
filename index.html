UniLion Ecosystem

UniLion's Token is built on top of an ecosystem where holders can participate collectively.

The DAO will be used to govern this ecosystem and built by the community, for the community.

The goal is that UniLion holders will maximise utility & useability.

UniLion

UniLion is a hyper-deflationary token powered by the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), which will be utilised in our community driven investment platform, LionPax. Holders will be able to vote, be rewarded for holding, and drive this community.

LionPax

We aim to bring the fractional ownership philosophy to the market - for every asset class, and lower the barrier of entry for pricey investments. Whether it's a "First Ever Tweet" NFT, a 1st Edition Charizard, or even a house, we will have you covered. Leave it to us to manage, and get your share of something big.

UniPride

Safety comes in numbers, and with Crypto it's no different. By becoming a member of the UniPride, you will be rewarded with voting rights to spearhead upcoming initiatives. UniPride members will be rewarded from our Community Pool, which holds a whopping 47.5% of tokens. Be a part of the giveaways, airdrops, competitions, & more. But most importantly, have your say
