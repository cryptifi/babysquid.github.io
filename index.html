What is UniLion?

UniLion ($LION) is a deflationary DeFi token on the Binance Smart Chain, which will be used on our platform driven by the community.

With UniLion, holders will collectively Create, Hold, and Invest in NFTs & Other Assets - reducing risk and maximising potential.

UniLion Ecosystem

UniLion is built on an ecosystem where holders can participate collectively. The DAO will be used to govern this ecosystem which is built by the community, for the community.

The goal is that UniLion holders will maximise utility & useability.

UniLion

UniLion ($LION) is a hyper-deflationary token powered by the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), which will be utilised in our community driven investment platform, LionPax. Holders will be able to vote, be rewarded for holding, and drive this community.

LionPax

LionPax will be a one-stop platform for fractional ownership investments. We aim to provide Users with transaction & asset management, DAO Voting, and social integrations.

UniPride

By becoming a member of the UniPride, you will be rewarded with voting rights to spearhead upcoming initiatives. UniPride members will be rewarded from our Community Pool, which holds a massive 47.5% of tokens. Be a part of the giveaways, airdrops, competitions, & more. But most importantly, have your say.

The UniLion Platform

We aim to bring the fractional ownership philosophy to the market - for every asset class, and lower the barrier of entry for pricey investments. Whether it's a "First Ever Tweet" NFT, a 1st Edition Charizard, or even a house, we will have you covered. Leave it to us to manage, and get your share of something big.

Invest Collectively

UniLion aims to provide investors access to high priced assets, through the shared ownership model. $LION Token holders can vote on a selection of assets, invest, and sell as a collective.
All future projects, new features, asset buying considerations, and fund allocations will be decided by UniPride.

UniPride NFT Minting

Every 3 days, community members can submit NFT proposals. This can be an image, music, 3D render - anything that can be represented digitally.
The submissions will be open for voting on our DAO platform.
The submission with the highest votes will be minted by UniLion, with the rights going to the submitter. The owner can decide to hold, sell on an open market, or sell back to UniPride.

Social & Community

LionPax aims to promote transparency by displaying all assets held by the community in a gallery. All assets & holders will be visible, with their share, the full purchase price, and current votes to sell.
The larger goal is to promoting visibility of holdings and the community to external entities.
